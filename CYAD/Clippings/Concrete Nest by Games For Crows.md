Wander the lands, scavenging comforting words with which to build your nest. A concrete poetry tool for gathering and arranging thoughts.

If you like, you can [share your nest](http://twitter.com/share?hashtags=concretenest) with us on twitter, or [view the collection](https://twitter.com/search?q=%23concretenest).

We're also building a small [community](https://www.reddit.com/r/concretenest/) over on reddit.  

Thank you for nesting with us <3
